# 🌌 Quantum Observer 🌌
> *"Do not look away, or the wavefunction might collapse and kill you."*

## 🌀 About The Game
Welcome to **Quantum Observer**, a purely client-side, post-quantum cryptographic, logic-bending survival game that lives right inside your browser! Written in pure JavaScript (Vanilla JS, CSS3, HTML5) with zero dependencies, this game bends the laws of physics and pseudo-random generation to deliver an immersive and unique experience.

As an Observer, you must navigate a collapsing universe where your very gaze defines reality. Anything outside your observation radius essentially ceases to exist. 

### ✨ Features
* **Wavefunction Collapse Engine:** The world only loads and iterates where you look! Turn your back, and things despawn into the quantum void.
* **Quantum Enemies & Entropy:** Enemies don't just chase you—they walk via stochastic, hash-guided quantum algorithms, occasionally teleporting in space.
* **Learning With Errors (LWE) Challenges:** The only way to survive is solving math and cryptographic puzzles to decode reality grids before they crush you.
* **Cryptography-infused Combat:** Weapons have deterministic entropies. Some shoot, some orbit you indefinitely until they strike an enemy. Every kill drops a hash in the real-time *Quantum Terminal*.
* **Random Seed Mode:** Try locking the universe down to a single Random Seed hash! Enemies, gold, and your fate will be entirely deterministic based on that one master key. 

## 🚀 How to Play
1. Simply open `index.html` in your favorite modern browser. You can even host it via a local server (`npx serve` or any HTTP server).
2. Use **WASD** to walk the grid.
3. Use the **Mouse** to look around and throw weapons (Click!).
4. Hit **R** to activate orbital weapons that you pick up. Space to stash them.
5. Watch your **Quantum Luck**! Let the terminal be your guide.
